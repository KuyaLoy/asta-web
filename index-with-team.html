About ASTA

ASTA is a future-ready virtual asset payment platform powered by blockchain technology. As a blockchain-based payment platform, ASTA also boosts the reliability of legacy methods, including cash and credit card payments. We are building a multiplexed ecosystem by expanding our operations to provide more than just marketing platforms to companies from various sectors, including hospitality, healthcare, retail, education, and more. By equipping enterprises with an efficient payment system that lowers fees for traditional payment methods, ASTA empowers businesses with market competitiveness.

The Shield represents ASTA’s reliability and safety. It reflects our vision to provide a secure payment platform for virtual assets and conventional currency users. With the two shades of blue, the ASTA symbol exudes creativity, innovation, and readiness for the future.

ASTA Bridge

ASTA Bridge is a swapping platform that enables users to send their ASTA tokens from one blockchain network to another. Furthermore, ASTA Bridge allows ASTA holders to seamlessly transfer their cryptocurrencies between Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and vice versa.
